ABOUT US

Prosperus Capital is engaged in raising funds of the European Union residents and investors from Eastern Europe. The funds are invested in securities, bonds and other financial instruments. Our company has a successful experience in co-development of commercial and residential real estate projects in Estonia.

We offer investors the best possible value, generating growth in the value of carefully selected real estate investments.

Prosperus Capital is acting as an intermediary in trading operations, providing both: goods’ supplier and a buyer with additional security.

Our mission is gaining maximum profit with a minimum risk.
